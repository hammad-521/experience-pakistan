Privacy Policy

Welcome to Experience Pakistan! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information from and about users of our website and the services offered thereon.

1. Information We Collect:

We collect information from you in several ways when you use the Website and Services:

  1. Information you provide: This includes information you submit during account creation, event listing, registration, or when you contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, location information, and any other information you choose to provide.
  2. Information collected automatically: We may collect certain information automatically when you use the Website and Services. This may include your IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, browsing history on our platform, and search queries.

2. Use of Information:

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  1. Providing and improving the Services: We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the Website and Services. This includes processing event listings, registrations, and facilitating communication between event organizers and attendees.
  2. Account management: We use your information to manage your account and provide customer support.
  3. Sending you communications: We may use your information to send you emails about your account, event updates, promotional offers, and other relevant information. You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails we send.

3. Sharing of Information:

We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate the Website and Services. These service providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, to enforce our Terms & Conditions, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of ourselves or others.

4. Data Security:

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you provide from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is completely secure.

5. Cookies:

We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect and store certain information. Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may limit your ability to use certain features of the Website and Services.

6. Your Choices:

You have choices regarding your information:

  1. Accessing and Updating Your Information: You can access and update your account information through your account settings.
  2. Contact Preferences: You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails we send.

7. Links to Other Websites:

Our Website and Services may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of other websites you visit.

8. Changes to this Policy:

We may revise this Privacy Policy at any time by posting the revised Policy on the Website. You are responsible for checking the Privacy Policy periodically for updates.

9. Contact Us:

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at experiencepakistan@gmail.com.
